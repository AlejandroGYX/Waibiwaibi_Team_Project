About Rainbow six

"Rainbow Six" is a well-known series of shooting simulation games owned by Ubisoft, with several generations of corresponding works. Its platform includes the host and mobile client. At the same time, the game supports multiplayer online collaboration tasks (Co-Op) or confrontation. The first work was released in August 1998.

The theme of the game "Rainbow Six" is adapted from the novel of the same name by the late famous military novelist Tom Clancy. The publisher Ubisoft also obtained the exclusive naming rights for all military games under Tom Clancy. . The game describes and plays a multinational cooperative anti-terrorist special force called "Rainbow", which performs various corresponding combat missions from their perspective to combat the harm of terrorism to the world. The name "Rainbow Six" itself is the code name of John Clark, the founder of the "Rainbow" team, according to the description of the original novel.
