Ahout APEX

"Apex Heroes" is a tactical competitive game developed by Respawn, the production team of "Titanfall". Released on February 5, 2019. Players will play the role of star warriors on the alien battlefield in the game.

"Apex Heroes" will land on the Nintendo Switch and Steam platforms in the fall of 2020, and supports PS4/Xbox One/Nintendo Switch/Steam/Origin cross-platform online.

"Apex Heroes" has a total of 8 legendary characters. Three players form a team. The first season will start in March, and season tickets will be launched. Each season will bring new weapons, characters and skins.
